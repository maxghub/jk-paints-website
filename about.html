About JK Paints

Driving automotive excellence with premium paint solutions since 2010. Quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction define our journey.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, JK Paints began with a vision to revolutionize the automotive paint industry in India.

What started as a small manufacturing unit in Noida has grown into one of the most trusted names in automotive paints. Our founder, Mr. Rajesh Kumar, an industry veteran with over 20 years of experience, identified the need for high-quality, durable automotive paints that could withstand Indian weather conditions.

Today, we serve over 5,000 clients across India, from individual workshops to large dealerships and fleet operators. Our commitment to innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction has made us a preferred choice for professionals who demand the best.
