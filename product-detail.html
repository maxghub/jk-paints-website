Application Guide

Surface Preparation:

  1. Clean surface thoroughly to remove dirt, grease, and wax
  2. Sand surface with 400-600 grit sandpaper
  3. Apply suitable primer if required
  4. Ensure surface is completely dry before application

Mixing Instructions:

  1. Shake paint container thoroughly before use
  2. Mix paint and hardener in ratio 2:1
  3. Add 10-15% thinner for proper viscosity
  4. Stir well for 2-3 minutes
  5. Let mixture stand for 10 minutes before application

Application:

  1. Use HVLP spray gun with 1.3-1.5 mm nozzle
  2. Apply 2-3 thin coats with 15-20 minutes flash-off time
  3. Maintain spray distance of 20-25 cm
  4. Apply at ambient temperature of 20-30°C
  5. Relative humidity should be below 80%

Drying & Curing:

  • Touch Dry: 30 minutes at 25°C
  • Handle Dry: 4-6 hours
  • Full Cure: 24 hours at 25°C
  • Force Dry: 60°C for 30 minutes (optional)
Safety Precautions: Use in well-ventilated area. Wear appropriate PPE including gloves, goggles, and respirator. Keep away from heat sources and open flames.
